    Nekopara Extra

    Nekopara Extra is an additional episode within the Nekopara visual novel series. It takes us back to the early days when Chocola and Vanilla, the lovable catgirls, were still kittens. Explore their bonding with their human master, Kashou, and their heartwarming experiences, including their first Christmas.

    Nekopara Vol. 0

    Nekopara Vol. 0 serves as a fan disc for Nekopara Vol. 1. It acts as a prequel to the original game, taking place just before Kashou opens his patisserie, La Soleil. The story revolves around the Minaduki household, focusing on the daily activities of Shigure (the eldest daughter) and the six catgirls. From waking up their master to preparing m eals and spending time together, it offers a glimpse into their leisurely lives. Expect heartwarming moments, mishaps in the kitchen (including cat food breakfasts), and the catgirls’ endearing interactions.

    Nekopara Vol. 1

    “Nekopara Vol. 1” is a visual novel that takes place in a world where humans live alongside catgirls. The protagonist, Kashou Minaduki, comes from a long line of Japanese confection makers and decides to open his own patisserie called "La Soleil". Upon moving out, he discovers two catgirls, Chocola and Vanilla, that his family had been raising, hidden amongst his belongings. Despite his initial attempts to send them back, they convince him to let them stay and they open La Soleil together, leading to a heartful comedy filled with their love for their master and occasional mishaps.

    Buy on Steam

    Nekopara Vol. 2

    “Nekopara Vol. 2” continues the story of Kashou Minaduki, the owner of the patisserie “La Soleil”, and his family of catgirls. This game focuses on Azuki and Coconut, two of the catgirl sisters. Despite their close relationship, a misunderstanding drives a wedge between them. As the story unfolds, they grow to understand each other better, strengthening their familial bonds in this heartfelt comedy.

    Buy on Steam

    Nekopara Vol. 3

    “Nekopara Vol. 3” is the fourth game in the series and focuses on the catgirls Maple and Cinnamon. The story unfolds in the patisserie “La Soleil”, where Maple encounters something that shakes her belief in attaining her dream. Cinnamon, who is very close to Maple, struggles to find a way to help her. The game is a heartwarming comedy about their ambitions, the bonds between family members, and their journey to overcome personal obstacles.

    Buy on Steam

    Nekopara Vol. 4

    “Nekopara Vol. 4” revolves around the protagonist Kashou Minaduki, who is struggling to meet his father’s expectations as a patisserie. Despite the popularity of “La Soleil”, his father continues to disapprove of it. Determined to gain his father’s approval, Kashou travels to France to seek advice from the teacher who taught him his craft. This journey introduces a new catgirl and brings about a new chapter in the NEKOPARA series

    Buy on Steam

    Nekopara Catboys Paradise

    “Nekopara Catboys Paradise” is a visual novel where you inherit your late grandfather’s café. To help you run the café, a school sends four trainees who are not humans, but humanoid cats. This is your first time interacting with humanoid cats, and you work alongside these four catboys. The game unfolds as a heartwarming story of your journey with these catboys in keeping your grandfather’s café running.
