Nekopara Visual Novels

"Nekopara" is a Japanese adult visual novel series developed by Neko Works. The series primarily focuses on the adventures of Kashou Minaduki, a young man who aspires to become a successful pastry chef and runs his own bakery called "La Soleil." The unique twist in the series is the presence of humanoid catgirls, who are treated as both pets and family members in the world of "Nekopara."

Nekopara Anime

In the heartwarming world of Nekopara, six adorable catgirls work at a confection shop run by their human owner, Kashou Minaduki. Their cozy lives take an unexpected turn when they encounter a tiny kitten. With a blend of comedy and slice-of-life moments, this anime celebrates the bond between feline companions and their master. You can watch it on Crunchyroll! 

Nekopara Ova

The Nekopara OVA is an Original Video Animation based on the popular Nekopara series. It follows the story of Kashou Minazuki, who moves away from home to open his patisserie, La Soleil. During unpacking, he discovers that two of his catgirls, Chocola and Vanilla, have stowed away in his luggage. The OVA combines comedy, romance, and the adorable antics of catgirls as they assist Kashou in making his dream a reality.

Nekopara Ova Extra

The Nekopara OVA Extra is an additional episode in the Nekopara visual novel series. It explores their early days at the Minazuki household, their gradual bonding with their human master, Kashou, and their heartwarming experiences, including their first Christmas.

Studio NekoWorks

NekoWorks is a Japanese doujin circle founded by the talented artist Sayori.NekoWorks primarily engages in creating galgames (visual novels) and related content. NekoWorks is best known for the NEKOPARA series, which features adorable catgirls and heartwarming stories set in a patisserie. They’ve also worked on series like MONMUSU and Tropical Liquor and LOVE³ -Love Cube- .
