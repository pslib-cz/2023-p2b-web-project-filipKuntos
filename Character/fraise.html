    Fraise

    With a penchant for strawberry sweets, she’s both talented and shy, relying on the trust of other.

    Fraise works at La Soleil as a waitress. Despite her talent, she’s shy and lacks confidence in her ingenuity. Fraise’s name is inspired by the French word for strawberry, reflecting the joy she found in sweet memories during her kittenhood.
