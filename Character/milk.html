Milk

A cheerful and sociable catgirl who helps run a takoyaki shop with her owner.

Milk is cheerful, polite, and sociable, addressing other catgirls as “older sisters.” She was a stray like Chocola and Vanilla, and her owner treats her like her own daughter. In Nekopara Vol. 4, she has grown up and wears a golden bell.
